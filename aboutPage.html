About Us

Welcome to our website! We are a group of five UO students who are trying to help unhoused people or people in need find safe sleeping locations. Our goal is to create a comprehensive database of safe sleeping locations in the area, and to make the data easy to understand and access.

At our core, we believe that everyone deserves a safe and comfortable place to sleep, regardless of their circumstances. We're working hard to create a more inclusive and compassionate community, where everyone has access to the resources and support they need.

Thank you for taking the time to learn more about our project.
