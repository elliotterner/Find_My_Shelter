Emergency Cold Weather Shelters

Eugene/Springfield Adults and Unaccompanied Youth: If temperatures are expected to drop below 30 degrees, Egan Warming Center Center will be activated to offer a secure refuge from the cold weather. Since there isn't a central gathering spot this year, it's essential to check the warming center locations for every activation. You can enroll online to receive alerts about Egan activation.

Eugene/Springfield Families: During Egan Warming Center activation nights, Catholic Community Services offers motel vouchers to families. However, to receive a voucher, families need to pre-register with the program. To sign up, you can call 541-345-3628 ext. 416 from Monday to Friday, 8 am-2 pm, or visit Catholic Community Services in Eugene (1464 W 6th Ave) or Springfield (1025 G Street).

Rural Lane County: Rural communities including Veneta and Florence are using apllet shelters this winter to provide additional capacity to already exisiting warming center operations. Households with children 17 or under in Florence, Oregon can contact Siuslaw Outreach services to inquire about motel vouchers on cold nights. Call 541-997-2816 or visit in person at 1576 West 12th St, Florence Monday through Thursday, 10 am-3:30 pm or Friday by appointment. During evenings or weekends call 541-997-4444.

Additionally, Cottage Grove is offering a warming shelter in partnership with Beds for Freezing Nights and Community Sharing.
